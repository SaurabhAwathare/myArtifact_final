# ADR-0007: Repository Ownership Model

## Status
Approved

## Date
2026-06-25

## Context
The project uses multiple repositories (`RecordingRepository`, `DraftRepository`, `EngagementRepository`, etc.).

## Problem
Undefined boundaries between repositories lead to circular dependencies and confusion about which repository should handle a specific transaction.

## Decision
Repositories are the sole owners of business transactions and data orchestration for their domain. Services and ViewModels must go through repositories for all persistence operations.

## Alternatives Considered
- **Shared DAOs in ViewModels**: Rejected as it bypasses business logic.

## Consequences
- Clean separation of concerns.
- Centralized business logic.
